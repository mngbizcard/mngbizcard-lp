MNG BizCard

Privacy-First Business Card Manager App

MNG BizCard is a minimalist contact manager for freelancers and professionals. It works entirely offline—no cloud storage, no external servers, no social links. Your data stays on your device.

Key Features

  • OCR text recognition (Google ML Kit)
  • Local storage only (SQLite). CSV / vCard import & export
  • Categories & fast search
  • Biometric app lock, optional encryption
  • Multilingual UI (JA / EN / ZH / ZH-TW / KO / HI)
