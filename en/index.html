MNG BizCard

Privacy-First Business Card Manager App

MNG BizCard is a minimalist business card manager app designed for freelancers and professionals who value privacy.

All your data stays completely offline — no cloud servers, no external syncing, and no data sharing.

Main Features

  • OCR text recognition (Google ML Kit)
  • Local data storage only (SQLite)
  • Contact categorization & search
  • CSV & vCard import/export
  • Biometric lock & optional encryption
  • Multi-language UI (JA / EN / ZH / ZH-TW / KO / HI)

Light ads only. You can support development through Ko-fi.
